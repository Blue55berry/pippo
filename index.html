Decadent Brownie

Brownie cake is a delicious dessert that combines the rich, fudgy texture of brownies with the celebratory feel of a cake. It's usually made by baking a thick layer of brownie batter in a round or square cake pan, then optionally frosting or decorating it like a cake.
